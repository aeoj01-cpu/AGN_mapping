## Current Status

Current work has successfully demonstrated:

- Generation of stochastic AGN continuum light curves.
- Construction of accretion disk and BLR transfer functions.
- Convolution of continuum light curves with transfer functions.
- Recovery of lag-frequency spectra using Fourier techniques.
- Initial investigation of wavelength dependence and BLR contributions.
- Testing of interpolation methods (including PyROA) for irregularly sampled light curves.

The numerical framework is now largely complete and can be used to investigate physical parameter dependencies.
